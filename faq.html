What Is A Commission Only Sales Agent?

A sales agent is a self-employed individual who engages with companies to sell the company’s products or services for a commission. The sales agent is not employed directly like an employee but acts like an outsourcing service to the company.

Sales agents are also known as manufacturers’ agents, independent business development managers and freelance sales reps.

Because commission-only sales agents only earn when they make a sale, they are results focused and driven to make the best use of their time gaining sales.
